shahi paneer

Shahi Paneer can literally be translated to “royal paneer.”
My recipe is that of a rich, creamy curry made with a base of nuts, onions, yogurt and spices.

Dal Makni

It is one of the most popular lentil recipes from North Indian Punjabi cuisine and is made with whole black lentils (urad dal or kali dal in Hindi) and kidney beans (rajma in Hindi).
This Dal Makhani Recipe is a restaurant style version with subtle
smoky flavors and creaminess of the lentils.
